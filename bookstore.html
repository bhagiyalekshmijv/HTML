The Alchemist - Paulo Coelho
The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho is a timeless story about following your dreams. It follows a young shepherd named Santiago on a journey to discover his true purpose, teaching readers about destiny, courage, and listening to one's heart.
Sherlock Holmes Series - Arthur Conan Doyle
A classic collection of mystery stories following the brilliant detective Sherlock Holmes and his loyal friend Dr. Watson as they solve intriguing crimes using sharp observation, logic, and deduction. Perfect for readers who love suspense, clever twists, and timeless detective fiction.
Pride and Prejudice - Jane Austen
Pride and Prejudice is a timeless romantic classic that follows the lively Elizabeth Bennet and the reserved Mr. Darcy as they navigate love, misunderstandings, and societal expectations. With wit, charm, and keen observations of human nature, the novel beautifully explores pride, prejudice, and the journey toward true undersyanding and love.
